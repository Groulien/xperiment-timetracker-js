This webapplication is a simple time tracker.
Information is stored on your computer, no information is sent to the internet.
Technically speaking, this is achieved via local storage. Clearing cookies may clear this as well.
Download in Excel/CSV format.
Note: Adjust the time column 'type format' to convert numbers to date format.

Timer

The timer allows you to start and stop a passive timer.
You can close this tab after starting the timer. Going back to this page will allow you to stop it.
